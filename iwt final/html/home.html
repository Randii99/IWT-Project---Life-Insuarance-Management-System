Insurance Types We Offer


Whole Life Insurance

Whole life insurance provides the insured with a guaranteed death benefit for the rest of
his or her life. Whole life insurance, in addition to paying a death benefit, has a
savings feature where cash value will grow tax-free. These are
often referred to as "traditional" life insurance plans.

Term Life Insurance

Term insurance is a form of life insurance policy that covers you for a certain amount
of time, or "term" of years. A death insurance may be compensated
if the insured dies within the policy's time span and the
policy is active, or in effect.
